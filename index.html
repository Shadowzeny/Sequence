How to Play Sequence

Objective

Be the first player to create 2 sequences of 5 tokens in a row!

Game Setup

  • You start with 7 cards in your hand
  • The board has 100 spaces with playing cards
  • Corner spaces are wild cards (can be used with any card)
  • Green tokens are yours, red tokens belong to the AI

How to Play

  1. Select a card from your hand by clicking on it
  2. Matching spaces on the board will highlight in yellow
  3. Click on a highlighted space to place your token
  4. You can place tokens on any space that matches your selected card
  5. Wild cards (corners with üÉè) can be used with any card

Winning

  • Create sequences of 5 tokens in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  • Wild cards count as part of any sequence
  • First player to create 2 sequences wins!
  • Sequences are marked automatically when formed

Game Controls

  • Draw Card: Take a new card from the deck (max 7 cards)
  • New Game: Start fresh with standard board layout
  • Reset Game: Clear tokens but keep same board layout
  • Randomize Board: Generate a new random board layout
